Design Decolonization

Welcome to Design Decolonization! This website is an interactive space where we will go through the connections between design and colonialism in India. This site is meant to be a low-stress informational module for those interested in learning more about the intersectionality between these two areas. Once you are done with a page, you can click the next button at the bottom right corner to proceed to the next slide.
